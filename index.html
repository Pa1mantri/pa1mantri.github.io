VSDMemSOC — Final GDSII After Routing
Final GDSII output after completed place-and-route of the VSDMemSOC design. The chip layout clearly shows three distinct regions: the I/O pad ring at the chip boundary (containing GPIO, and power), the SRAM hard macro (a pre-built memory block placed inside the core area), and the synthesized standard cell logic densely packed and routed throughout the remaining core area around the SRAM macro. The design is ready for foundry submission — generated via the OpenLane automated PnR flow.
